The solution

We spent 6 months making an effort to wave more, whether we were right or wrong. Simple right? Sometimes a small change can have a huge impact.


Actions speak louder than words.
Just like a smile, waving shows mutual respect; easing tension and making journeys happier for everyone.

We want to grow this culture of goodwill to build a movement!

begins
with

Glove makes waving fun and meaningful.

Super visible, front & back.
An excuse to wave more often.

Wearing Glove shows you are invested in
a cause, and builds respect.
